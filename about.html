Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

To rent a car, you'll need a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name, and a form of identification (passport or ID card). For international customers, an International Driving Permit may be required.

What is your fuel policy?

We operate a full-to-full fuel policy. This means you'll receive the car with a full tank and are expected to return it with a full tank. If the car is returned with less fuel, a refueling fee will be charged.

Is there a minimum age requirement?

Yes, the minimum age to rent a standard car is 21 years. For luxury and premium vehicles, the minimum age is 25 years. Young driver surcharges may apply for drivers under 25.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation up to 48 hours before the scheduled pickup time without any penalty. For changes or cancellations within 48 hours, a fee may apply.

Do you offer insurance coverage?

Yes, we offer various insurance options including Collision Damage Waiver (CDW), Theft Protection (TP), and Personal Accident Insurance (PAI). Basic insurance is included in all rentals, with options to upgrade for more comprehensive coverage.
